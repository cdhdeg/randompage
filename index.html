Welcome to the all-new Dukes Hub!

We are excited to announce that the new Hub will officially launch in September 2024. Training on how to use the new platform will be provided during one of your INSET days at the beginning of the term.

Since you’ve found your way here early we encourage you to log in and explore the platform. You can start booking courses now and participate in active community areas.

Our goal is to make the sign-in process as smooth as possible allowing you to access our incredible courses without any hassle. We’ve collaborated with your institution’s IT team to connect your school email address to our system.

Easy Sign-In with Microsoft

If your institution uses Microsoft, signing into the Dukes Hub is straightforward. Simply click the button below to be directed to the SSO login page.

Easy Sign-In with Google

If your institution uses Google you will need to register your account first. Don't worry—you won’t need to remember a new username or password. Just follow these steps:

Click the SSO sign-in button then select the "Create account" link.

Click "Sign up with Google."

Enter your institution’s email address and password or if you are already logged in to your browser simply select your account name.

Your Google account is now linked to Dukes Hub and you can use it to log in.

We hope you enjoy exploring the new Dukes Hub!
